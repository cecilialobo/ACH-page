23% of the urban population in South Africa is living in informal housing settlements.


You can do something about it.

The Initiative

The situation of the South African population who lives in informal settlements is precarious. There is no sanitation or plumbing, no running water and no eletricity. In the last 10 years, urbanization has increased the number of South Africans relying on informal accommodations. Considering all that, and the fact that there are more than 14 million ex-service containers in the world, ACH decided to take action. Join us in this mission to bring housing to those who need it most.
